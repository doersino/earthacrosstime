Optician Sans is a free typeface based on the 10 historical optotype letters seen on millions of eye charts around the world, finalising the work that was started decades ago.
 
Optician Sans is created by ANTI Hamar together with typographer Fábio Duarte Martins.

Optician Sans is released under the SIL OPEN FONT LICENSE: https://opensource.org/licenses/OFL-1.1

More information on optician-sans.com.
